# Club Manager

The Club Manager is where you can store and manage **club and player profiles** that persist across multiple matches. Instead of re-entering teams and rosters from scratch every game, you can save them here and load them quickly in [Match Setup](Match-Setup).

---

## Opening the Club Manager

From the Home Dashboard, click **"Club Manager"**.

---

## Creating a Club

1. Click **"Add Club"** or the + button.
2. Enter the **Club Name** (e.g. "Oost Amsterdam").
3. Set the **Club Color** — this is the default color used for the team in all views.
4. Optionally upload a **Club Logo** (used in overlays and statistics).
5. Click **Save**.

---

## Adding Players to a Club

Once a club is created, click on it to open its detail view. Here you can manage the roster:

1. Click **"Add Player"**.
2. Enter the player's:
   - **Name**
   - **Jersey Number**
   - **Gender** (Male / Female)
   - **Position** (optional)
3. Click **Save**.

Players are saved to the club and will be available any time you load that club in Match Setup.

---

## Using a Club in Match Setup

When you are on the [Match Setup](Match-Setup) screen:
- Click **"Load Club"** for either the Home or Away team.
- Select the club from your saved list.
- The team name, color, and full player roster are pre-filled automatically.

This can save several minutes of data entry, especially for teams you play against or with regularly.

---

## Editing and Deleting

- Click any club to edit its name, color, or logo.
- Click any player within a club to edit their details.
- Delete a player or a whole club using the delete (trash) button.

> **Note:** Deleting a club only removes it from the Club Manager — it does **not** affect any already-recorded match data.

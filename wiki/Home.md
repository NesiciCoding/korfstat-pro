## 🌐 Multi-Device Support

KorfStat Pro is designed to work across **multiple devices simultaneously**. You can open different views on different screens, tablets, or laptops — and they all stay in sync in real time. This means you can have:

- A laptop running the **Match Tracker** for recording goals and events.
- A tablet running the **Jury View** for timekeeping.
- A TV running the **Live Stats View** for spectators.
- A computer running the **Stream Overlay** for your livestream.

All connected over your local Wi-Fi network, automatically synchronized.

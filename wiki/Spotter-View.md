# Spotter View

The Spotter View is a simplified input screen designed for a **dedicated stats spotter** — someone whose only job is to record specific events during the match (like shots or fouls) quickly, without needing access to the full Match Tracker.

---

## Opening This View

From the Home Dashboard, click **"Spotter"** or navigate to:
```
http://[your-ip]:3000/?view=SPOTTER
```

---

## What it's For

In professional or semi-professional settings, it can be useful to have one person dedicated to running the clocks (Jury View) and another person dedicated to recording shots and stats (Spotter View). This reduces workload on either operator.

The Spotter View provides:
- A simplified interface to quickly log shots and events.
- Input is synced to the main Match Tracker in real time.

---

## How to Use

1. Open the Spotter View on a tablet or second laptop.
2. Use the on-screen buttons to record events as they happen.
3. All logged events appear immediately in the Match Tracker and Statistics View.

---

## Tips

- The Spotter View is ideal for someone watching from a different vantage point (e.g. the stands, or a different camera angle from the main operator).
- Use a tablet with a good viewing angle of the field for the most accurate shot location tracking.

# Match Analysis (AI)

The Match Analysis view uses **artificial intelligence** (powered by Google Gemini) to generate automated insights, commentary, and tactical analysis for any recorded match.

---

## Requirements

This feature requires a **Google Gemini API key** to be configured. See [Installation & Setup](Installation-and-Setup) for how to obtain and configure your key.

> Without an API key, the rest of the app works perfectly. Match Analysis is an optional bonus feature.

---

## Opening Match Analysis

You can reach the Match Analysis view in two ways:
1. From the **Statistics View** — click the **"Analyze (AI)"** button after a match.
2. From **Match History** — click the **"Analyze"** button next to any saved match.

---

## What the AI Generates

### Match Summary
A written narrative summary of the match — like a journalist's match report. Covers the key moments, turning points, and final result in plain language.

### Goal Breakdown
An analysis of how each goal was scored, where on the field, and at what point in the game.

### Tactical Observations
Observations about each team's shooting patterns, defensive pressure, and overall game strategy based on the recorded event data.

### Player Spotlights
Highlights from individual top performers during the match — who scored, who created chances, and who was most active.

### AI Commentary Lines
A set of ready-to-use commentary lines that a broadcaster could use for social media, post-match posts, or video content.

---

## How to Use the Analysis

1. Open the analysis for a match.
2. The AI will process the match data (it may take a few seconds).
3. Read the generated content in the sections on screen.
4. You can **copy** sections to paste into a match report, newsletter, or social post.

---

## Choosing the AI Model

In [Settings](Settings), under **Integrations**, you can choose which Gemini model to use:

| Model | Description |
|---|---|
| Gemini 2.0 Flash (Experimental) | Fastest, cutting edge |
| Gemini 2.0 Flash (Stable) | Fast and reliable |
| Gemini 1.5 Flash | Good fallback option |
| Gemini 1.5 Pro | Highest intelligence, slower |

For most users, **Gemini 2.0 Flash (Stable)** is the best balance of speed and quality.

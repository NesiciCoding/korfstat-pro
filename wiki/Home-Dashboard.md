# Home Dashboard

The Home Dashboard (also called the Landing Gateway) is the **main hub** of KorfStat Pro. It is the first screen you see when the app loads, and it gives you a central place to navigate to every feature in the application.

---

## What You See on the Home Screen

The Home Dashboard is divided into several sections:

### 🎮 Active Match Status
If a match is currently in progress (or was paused), the dashboard shows a summary card. You can:
- See the current score at a glance.
- Click to **resume** the active match in the Match Tracker.

### 🚀 Start New Match
The most important button — click **"New Match"** to go to the [Match Setup](Match-Setup) screen and configure the next game.

### 📺 Live Views
A section of quick-launch buttons to open the various display and broadcast views:
- **Live Stats (Big Screen)** — open a scoreboard for spectators.
- **Jury View** — open the table official view.
- **Livestream Stats** — open the commentary / streaming view.
- **Shot Clock** — open a standalone shot clock display.
- **Stream Overlay** — open the OBS-ready overlay.
- **Director Dashboard** — open the broadcast control console.
- **Spotter View** — open the spotter input tool.

### 📊 History & Analysis
Quick links to browse past data:
- **Match History** — see a list of all completed matches.
- **Overall Statistics** — view aggregated player and team performance.

### 🗺️ Planning Tools
- **Strategy Planner** — launch the tactical whiteboard.
- **Season Manager** — manage leagues, seasons, and standings.
- **Club Manager** — manage club and player data.

---

## Active Sessions Indicator

In the top area of the home screen, you may see a small indicator showing how many **active sessions** are currently connected (i.e., how many devices or browser tabs are open). This is useful to confirm that your multi-device setup is working.

---

## Navigation Tip

You can always return to the Home Dashboard by clicking the **"Home"** or **back** button found in most views. Your match data is saved automatically, so you won't lose anything by navigating around.

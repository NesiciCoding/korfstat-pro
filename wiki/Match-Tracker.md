# Match Tracker

The Match Tracker is the **heart of KorfStat Pro** — this is the screen you use during the game itself to record everything that happens.

---

## Overview

The Match Tracker is a live, interactive screen showing:
- The **Korfball field** where you record shot attempts.
- The **scoreboard** with both teams' scores.
- The **Game Clock** and **Shot Clock**.
- Controls for game events like substitutions, timeouts, and fouls.

---

## The Korfball Field

The field is displayed as an interactive map. Click or tap on the field to record **shot attempts** at the precise location where they took place.

### Recording a Shot
1. Select the **active team** (Home or Away) using the possession buttons.
2. Click/tap on the field at roughly the spot where the shot was taken.
3. A popup appears asking for the result:
   - **Goal** — the ball went in. The score is updated immediately.
   - **Saved / Missed / Blocked** — a failed attempt is recorded.
4. You can also select **which player** took the shot for individual statistics.

All shots are stored with their position, player, and result. This powers the **shot heatmaps** and analysis views.

---

## Managing the Game Clock

### Starting and Stopping
- The large **Game Clock** button starts and stops the official match timer.
- When the timer is running, it counts up from 0:00 towards the set half duration (e.g. 25:00).

### Adjusting the Time
If you need to correct the clock (e.g. it was started a few seconds late), you can tap the clock display to manually adjust the seconds.

---

## Managing the Shot Clock

The **Shot Clock** (25 seconds by default) operates independently of the Game Clock. This gives the timekeeper full flexibility.

- Press the **start/stop** button to control the shot clock.
- Press **Reset** to restart it at 25 seconds (e.g. after a goal or a foul).
- The shot clock **turns red** in the final 5 seconds as a warning.

> **Important:** Starting the Game Clock does NOT automatically start the Shot Clock. This is by design — it gives the jury official full control, as required by Korfball rules.

---

## Recording Game Events

### Goals
Goals are recorded automatically when you mark a shot as a goal on the field. The scoreboard updates in real-time across all connected devices.

### Fouls
Record fouls via the events panel. Select the player and the type of foul.

### Yellow and Red Cards (Disciplinary)
Use the card buttons to issue disciplinary cards. These are logged in the events and shown in statistics.

### Timeouts
Press the **Timeout** button for the relevant team. A 60-second timeout countdown will start, and connected devices (including the Wear OS watch) will receive a notification immediately.

---

## Substitutions

To record a substitution:
1. Press the **Substitution** button.
2. Select the player going **out** and the player coming **in**.
3. Confirm. The substitution is logged and all connected screens (including the referee's watch) receive a popup notification with the player names and numbers.

---

## Half Time

At the end of the first half:
1. Stop the clock.
2. Press **Half Time**. This resets the clock for the second half and swaps the Attack and Defense zones between teams (as per Korfball rules).

---

## Finishing the Match

When the final whistle blows:
1. Stop the clock.
2. Press **Finish Match**.
3. You will be taken to the [Statistics View](Statistics-View) with a full breakdown of the completed match.
4. The match is automatically saved to [Match History](Match-History).

---

## Undo Last Action

Made a mistake? Press the **Undo** button to reverse the most recent recorded event (goal, shot, foul, etc.).

> **Tip:** Use the Undo button quickly after a mistake. It only undoes the single last action.

---

## Navigating to Other Views During a Match

During a match, you can open other views without losing any data. Use the navigation buttons or menus to switch to:
- **Jury View** (for a dedicated timekeeper on another device).
- **Live Stats View** (to open the big screen scoreboard).
- **Statistics View** (to check current stats mid-match).

Your match state is synced across all devices automatically.

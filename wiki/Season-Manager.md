# Season Manager

The Season Manager lets you **organise your matches into seasons or leagues**, automatically calculate standings, and track your team's progress over a full competition.

---

## Opening the Season Manager

From the Home Dashboard, click **"Season Manager"** (trophy icon).

---

## Creating a New Season

1. Click the **"Create New Season"** card (with the + icon).
2. Type the name of the season, for example `2024/2025` or `Spring League`.
3. Press **Enter** or click **Create**.

Your new season appears as a card in the season list.

---

## Assigning Matches to a Season

When you set up a match in [Match Setup](Match-Setup), there is a dropdown at the top to **assign the match to a season**. Select the correct season before starting the match.

All matches assigned to a season are automatically counted in its standings.

---

## Viewing a Season

Click on any season card to open its detail view. Inside you will see:

### League Table
A standings table showing all teams that have played at least one match in this season:

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pos | League position (rank) |
| Team | Team name |
| P | Matches played |
| W | Matches won |
| D | Matches drawn |
| L | Matches lost |
| GF | Goals scored |
| GA | Goals conceded |
| +/- | Goal difference |
| Pts | Total points |

- **Win = 3 points**, **Draw = 1 point** each, **Loss = 0 points**.
- Teams are ranked by points, then by goal difference.

### Season Info Panel
Shows the start date of the season and how many matches have been recorded.

---

## Multiple Seasons

You can have as many seasons as you need — one for each league, cup, or training block. They are all stored independently and can be viewed anytime.

---

## Tips

- Create a season at the start of each competition or league so you always have a clean standings table.
- If you forget to assign a match to a season when setting it up, unfortunately it cannot be retroactively linked to a season (this is a current limitation).
- Seasons persist in the browser's local storage — export your match data regularly for safekeeping.

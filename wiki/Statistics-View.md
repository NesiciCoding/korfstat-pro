# Statistics View

The Statistics View shows you a **comprehensive breakdown** of a completed (or in-progress) match. You are taken here automatically after you finish a match, or you can access it from [Match History](Match-History).

---

## What Statistics Are Shown?

### Scoreboard Summary
At the top, you see the final (or current) score for each team in big, clear numbers.

### Shot Analysis
A visual display of all shot attempts, showing:
- **Total shots taken** by each team.
- **Goals scored** vs **attempts missed/saved/blocked**.
- **Shot accuracy percentage** for each team.

### Shot Heatmap / Position Map
A diagram of the korfball field showing **where all shots were taken** during the match, coded by result (goal vs miss). This helps identify which zones a team is shooting from and how effective they were from each area.

### Events Timeline
A chronological list of everything that happened in the match:
- Goals (with player name, time, and position)
- Failed shots
- Cards and fouls  
- Substitutions
- Timeouts
- Half time

### Player Statistics
A breakdown per player, showing:
- **Goals scored**
- **Shots taken**
- **Shooting accuracy**
- **Cards received**
- **Minutes played**

### Team Comparison
A side-by-side comparison table of both teams, covering all major statistics.

---

## Exporting Statistics

You can export the statistics in several formats:
- **PDF** — a formatted report suitable for printing or emailing.
- **Excel (CSV)** — a spreadsheet-friendly export for further analysis.
- **JSON** — raw data for developers or custom tools.

Click the **Export** button and choose your preferred format.

---

## Sharing on Social Media

The **Share** button lets you generate a shareable summary card (image) with the key match statistics — perfect for posting to your club's social media after the game.

---

## Navigating from Statistics View

From the Statistics View, you can:
- Click **Back** to return to the Match Tracker.
- Click **Home** to go to the Home Dashboard.
- Click **Analyze (AI)** to open the [Match Analysis](Match-Analysis) view for AI-powered insights.

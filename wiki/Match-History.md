# Match History

The Match History screen lets you browse all the matches you have recorded in KorfStat Pro. It acts as your personal match archive.

---

## Opening Match History

Access Match History from the **Home Dashboard** by clicking the "Match History" link, or use the navigation menu from within any view.

---

## What You'll See

A list of all completed matches, showing for each one:
- **Date** the match was played.
- **Teams** (Home vs Away) and the **final score**.
- The **season** it was part of (if assigned).

Matches are sorted with the most recent at the top.

---

## What You Can Do

### 📊 View Statistics
Click on any match in the list to open its [Statistics View](Statistics-View) and review the full breakdown: shots, goals, player performance, events timeline, and more.

### 🔬 Run AI Analysis
Click the **"Analyze"** button on any match to open the [Match Analysis](Match-Analysis) view, which uses AI to generate insights and commentary about the match.

### 🗑️ Delete a Match
If you no longer need a match record, click the **Delete** button (rubbish bin icon) next to it. You will be asked to confirm before it is permanently removed.

---

## Important: Where Is My Data Stored?

Match data is stored in your browser's **local storage**. This means:

- The data is saved on **this specific device and browser only**.
- If you clear your browser history or switch to a different browser or computer, you will lose your saved matches.
- **Always export important matches** as PDF, Excel, or JSON from the [Statistics View](Statistics-View) to create a permanent backup.

> **Tip:** After each important match, export a PDF report. It takes 10 seconds and guarantees you always have a copy.

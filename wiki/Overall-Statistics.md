# Overall Statistics

The Overall Statistics view gives you an **aggregated view of all recorded matches** — not just one game, but the cumulative picture of player and team performance over time.

---

## Opening Overall Statistics

From the [Home Dashboard](Home-Dashboard), click the **"Overall Stats"** button.

---

## What's Shown

### All-Time Leaderboard
A ranked list of players across all recorded matches, sorted by total goals scored. See who your best shooter is instantly.

### Team Performance Summary
For each team you have recorded data on:
- Total matches played.
- Total goals scored and conceded.
- Win / Draw / Loss record.

### Player Career Stats
Select any player to see their complete record across all matches:
- Total goals
- Total shots taken
- Overall shooting accuracy
- Cards received across all games

---

## Difference From Match History

| Match History | Overall Statistics |
|---|---|
| Shows one match at a time | Shows aggregated data across all matches |
| Full timeline of events | Top-level career stats per player/team |
| Good for reviewing a single game | Good for long-term analysis and scouting |

---

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of This View

- The more matches you record, the more meaningful the data becomes.
- Assign your matches to a [Season](Season-Manager) so you can filter stats by season.
- Export this data from the [Statistics View](Statistics-View) to share with coaches or players.

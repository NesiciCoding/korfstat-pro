# Strategy Planner (Whiteboard)

The Strategy Planner is an **interactive tactical whiteboard** where you can draw up plays, plan formations, and animate movement sequences using a digital Korfball field. Coaches can use this before or after a match for tactical discussions.

---

## Opening the Strategy Planner

From the Home Dashboard, click **"Strategy Planner"** or the whiteboard icon.

---

## The Korfball Field

The main area of the screen is a digital representation of a full Korfball field, drawn to scale (40m × 20m), with:
- Both posts (korf) clearly marked with orange penalty circles.
- The center dividing line.
- Light/dark mode support.

---

## Player Tokens

The field starts with **8 player tokens** pre-placed:
- **4 Home team players** (blue) in the attack zone.
- **4 Away team players** (red) in the defense zone.

Player tokens use **two different shapes** to distinguish gender:
- **Circles** = Male players.
- **Hexagons** = Female players.

### Moving Tokens
1. Select the **Move tool** (cursor icon) in the right toolbar.
2. Click and drag any token to reposition it on the field.
3. Tokens stay within the field boundaries.

### Editing Token Labels
Double-click any token to edit its label (up to 3 characters — e.g. a player's initials or jersey number).

---

## Drawing Tools

The right toolbar has several drawing tools:

| Tool | What It Does |
|---|---|
| 🖱️ Move / Select | Drag tokens around the field |
| ✏️ Pen | Draw freehand lines on the field |
| ➡️ Arrow | Draw lines with an arrowhead (great for showing movement direction) |
| 🧹 Clear | Remove all drawings from the current frame |

### Color Picker
Below the tools, choose from 4 preset colors: Red, Blue, Green, Yellow. Select a color before drawing.

---

## Multi-Frame Animation

The Strategy Planner supports **frame-by-frame tactical animation** — so you can show a play developing step by step.

### Working with Frames

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| Add a new frame | Press **+ Add Frame** in the bottom timeline bar |
| Switch between frames | Click a frame thumbnail in the timeline |
| Delete a frame | Hover over the frame and click the ✕ button |

### Playing Back the Animation
Press the **Play** button in the timeline controls to see all frames play one after another, giving a smooth animated view of the play.

---

## Saving and Loading Plays

### Saving a Play
Press the **"Save Play"** button (top right). A dialog will ask you to name the play (e.g. "Corner Play", "Fast Break Phase 1").

Plays are saved to your browser's local storage and appear in the **Saved Plays sidebar** on the left.

### Loading a Play
Click any play in the **Saved Plays** sidebar to load it. You will be asked to confirm, as unsaved changes to the current board will be lost.

### Deleting a Play
Hover over a saved play in the sidebar and click the rubbish bin icon.

---

## Resetting the Board

Press the **Reset** button to clear everything and start fresh with the default player positions. You will be asked to confirm.

---

## Tips for Coaches

- Use arrows in different colors to distinguish Home and Away movement patterns.
- Add multiple frames to show a ball movement sequence (pass, cut, shot).
- Name plays clearly (e.g. "2-1 Press", "Attack Overload Right") so they are easy to find later.
- Press **Play** during a team meeting to animate the play live.
